# Voiceover script — Channel Intro (v2, credibility-forward)

Read it like you talk — confident, a little casual, NOT stiff. Contractions, natural pace.
Pause a beat at each `—`. Bracketed cue = what's on screen. (Source of truth for timing is
`intro.data.ts`; captions get re-synced to your exact VO after you record.)

Record with the new mic (~4–6" away, slightly across it). Save to `public/channel-intro/`.
